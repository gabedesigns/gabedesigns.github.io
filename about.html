HEY, WHAT'S GOOD?

I'm Gabriel , a UX/UI Designer by day, korean drama simp by night. I love design for what it is, but I am most fueled by applying my creativity to product experiences.

Growing up, I was told that everything around us comes with a “why” which instilled in me a natural curiosity. I gravitate towards the idea of how everything we do should be meaningful. I am a vicarious thinker, often preferring a deep understanding of the intentions of others and “why” they do what they do.

I am an advocate for developing unique and intentional experiences for others, or as some may call it being a people person.

When I'm not tinkering with my designs, you can catch me bumping Drake in my car, taking or editing photos, and spending quality time with my loved ones.
